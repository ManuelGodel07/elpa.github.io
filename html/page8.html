B1 English (Intermediate)

Level B1 corresponds to users who can understand and produce text on familiar topics and give opinions and descriptions.

Skills at level B1

Please be confident you are B1, this is intermediate level. you can do the following: Understand the main points of clear texts in standard language if they are about topics with which they are familiar, whether in work, study or leisure contexts. Cope with most of the situations that might arise on a trip to areas where the language is used. Produce simple, coherent texts about topics with which they are familiar or in which they have a personal interest. Describe experiences, events, wishes and aspirations, as well as briefly justifying opinions or explaining plans.
